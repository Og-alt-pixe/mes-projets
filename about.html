Our Story

Founded in 2020 in Berlin, Studio X emerged from a desire to bridge the gap between digital design and traditional craftsmanship. We believe that the best work happens when precision meets passion, when code meets canvas.

Our approach combines the rigor of Swiss design principles with the warmth of artisanal techniques. Every project is an opportunity to explore this intersection, creating work that is both timeless and contemporary.
